History Of School

Universal Higher Secondary School, established in 2049 B.S., is the academic shrine for all meritorious and promising children who genuinely desire a meaningful life. One of the visions of this English medium school is to impart education to serve humanity because service to mankind is service to God. It dedicates itself to providing excellence, glory and militancy to students. It further aims at the all-round development, i.e. intellectual, physical, social and spiritual: a holistic approach of teaching. It helps students excavate best out of their life.

Vision

Universal HSS aims at becoming an internationally recognized academic institution for the excellent education.

Mission

As it's mission, it ever strives for generating quality students through the implementation of the holistic approach. It ever wishes its budding stars to be well furnished with the eastern heart and the western mind. Further, it's mission is to help in producing truly successful citizens with excellently enriched body, mind and soul.

Achievements

  • 100% result in the SLC for the past 10 years. Well-recognized by people for its outstanding discipline and excellent teaching-learning.
  • The 2nd highest marks in Management stream in the HSEB exam of 2061 B.S.
  • Number of shields, medals and certificates in co- and extra-curricular contests. vel-recognized for the excellent English environment.
